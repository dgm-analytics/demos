PRESCRIPTIVE INSIGHTS — APRIL 2026
DGM ANALYTICS — MONTHLY RECOMMENDATIONS
Footwear has a 14.2% return rate — nearly double your store average. That's $1,820 walking back out the door every month.
→ Review sizing info and product descriptions for footwear. A fit guide or size chart at point of sale could cut this in half.
14 SKUs have had zero sales in 60+ days and that number is growing. Dead inventory ties up cash and floor space.
→ Mark down the 5 oldest items by 20–30% this week. Recovered cash outperforms shelf space for slow-moving product every time.
Monday and Tuesday average under $4,000/day — roughly half of Thursday. You may be over-staffed on your two slowest days.
→ Shift one part-time shift from Mon/Tue to Fri/Sat where revenue is 3–4x higher. Estimated net margin gain: $800–$1,200/month.
Return customer rate is up 6pts over 6 months — 34% of your customers are choosing to come back. That's a real loyalty signal.
→ Introduce a simple repeat-customer incentive to push toward 40%. Repeat customers spend 25–40% more per visit on average.
